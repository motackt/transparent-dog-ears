Hidden Overflow

This method is probably the simplest, but doesn't allow for inner elements (like the purple button) to leave the post.

Background-Clip

This method allows elements to leave the post, useful for menus or other effects.
However it has the unfortunate issue of not being able to round the top-right corner.

Clip-Path

This method has the advantages of allowing elements to leave the post, while also maintaining a rounded corner!
However, it doesn't have great browser support at the time of making these methods.
